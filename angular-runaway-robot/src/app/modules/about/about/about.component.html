What is Runaway Robot?

A software engineer's personal project, and a game for people who like learning new things and solving puzzles.

Why did I make Runaway Robot?

As I child, I loved games that involved puzzles and problem solving. I grew up with games like the King's Quest series and the Carmen Sandiego Games.

Back then, I imagined re-creating those games with my own variations, but of course, I was 13 years old, with a very limitted skillset.

More than a decade later, I realized I now have the ability to fulfil that dream, and so I set out to create Runaway Robot.

I hope you enjoy the game. Thank you for joining me in this game. I wish you all the best in your quest for Raju the Runaway Robot.
